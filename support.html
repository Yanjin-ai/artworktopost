Quick help

How do I add my artwork?

On the Artwork tab, tap “Upload / replace current work” and choose a photo of your piece.

How do I make a series?

Add several works with the “＋” button, then open the Series tab to arrange them into a grid or mural and add a series statement.

How do I make a video?

Open the Story tab, pick a ratio, motion and rhythm, then tap “Export video”.

How do I add my signature?

In the Signature section you can type a name, draw your signature by hand, or import a photo of it.

Where are my images stored?

Everything stays on your device. Atelier Post has no account and no server — see our Privacy Policy.
