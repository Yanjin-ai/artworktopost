Privacy Policy

Atelier Post · Last updated 22 June 2026

Atelier Post is a tool for framing and organizing your own artwork into posts and videos. We built it to respect your work and your privacy. This policy explains, in plain terms, what the app does and does not do with your data.

The short version

We do not collect any personal data. Atelier Post has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. Everything you create stays on your device.

Where your data lives

  • Your artwork images, titles, captions, signatures and settings are stored only on your device, using the operating system's local storage and on-device database.
  • This information is never uploaded to us — we do not operate any server that receives your content. We never see, store, or have access to your images or text.

Photos and camera

  • The app asks for access to your photo library (and, optionally, the camera) only so you can choose or capture the artwork you want to work with. Those images stay on your device.
  • When you export or share, the app may save the finished image or video to your device's photo library and/or open the system share sheet so you can post it. This happens entirely on your device.

Analytics, advertising and tracking

None. We do not use any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking technologies, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
